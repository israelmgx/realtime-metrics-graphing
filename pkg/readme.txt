Almost done!!

1. Login to the up.time monitoring station interface, click on the "Config" tab and then click on "up.time Configuration".

2. Paste the following and click on Update button at the bottom:
realtime.view.enabled=true
realtime.view.URL=http://UPTIME-HOST/realtime/realtime.php?

(Note: Change "UPTIME-HOST" to the system hostname/IP where up.time is installed.)

Done.
